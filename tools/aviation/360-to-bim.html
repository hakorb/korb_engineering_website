Welcome to 360° to BIM

Transform 360° panoramas into professional BIM models. Here are some tips to get started:

  • Drop a 360° panorama onto the upload zone in Step 1
  • Press 1–5 to jump between workflow steps
  • Use Ctrl+S to save your project (also autosaves every 60s)
  • Press M for measure, A for annotate
  • Drag to orbit, right-click to pan, scroll to zoom
  • Right-click any element for a context menu
