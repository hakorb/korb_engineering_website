AVIATION COMPUTATIONAL KNOWLEDGE

[ + ] WHAT CAN I ASK?

Supports natural-language queries for common aviation calculations. Examples:

  • density altitude at 5000 ft and 30C
  • TAS 120 kias 8000 ft 10C — true airspeed from CAS/IAS
  • mach 0.78 at FL350 -50C — mach to TAS
  • wind triangle course 090 tas 120 wind 270 at 20
  • crosswind runway 18 wind 210 at 15
  • distance KDFW to KLGA — great-circle between airports
  • distance 34.5N 97.2W to 35.0N 96.5W — great-circle between coords
  • fuel burn 7 hours at 42 gph
  • endurance 80 gallons at 42 gph
  • time to climb 3000 ft at 500 fpm
  • top of descent 10000 ft to 2500 ft at 500 fpm at 180 kt
  • ISA temp at 8000 ft / ISA deviation 10C at 5000 ft
  • turn radius 180 kt standard rate / turn radius 180 kt at 30 bank
  • pressure altitude 5000 ft 29.82
  • climb gradient 800 fpm at 120 kt
  • cloud base 25C 18C dewpoint
  • convert 120 knots to mph

Units are forgiving: kt, kts, knots, kias, kcas, ktas, mph, ft, feet, m, meters, nm, nmi, sm, km, °C/C, °F/F, inHg, hPa/mb, gph, lbs, kg.
