"Or Approved Equal" Language

Federal procurement and FAA-funded projects require that specifications not be unduly restrictive. When a specific manufacturer or product is named, the phrase "or approved equal" must follow to permit competitive bidding.

Standard clause used by this tool:

"[Manufacturer] [Model], or approved equal. Substitution requests shall be submitted in accordance with Section 01 25 00 — Substitution Procedures."

Substitution requests typically require:

  • Written request at least 10 days prior to bid
  • Complete technical data and comparison
  • Certification that the substitute meets or exceeds specified performance
  • Acceptance by the Engineer of Record

Specification Structure (CSI 3-Part Format)

  • PART 1 — GENERAL: Scope, references, submittals, quality assurance, delivery/storage
  • PART 2 — PRODUCTS: Manufacturers, materials, performance requirements, fabrication
  • PART 3 — EXECUTION: Installation, field quality control, cleaning, closeout
