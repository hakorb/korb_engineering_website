About This Tool
Building, airports, pilots, drones, operators — the FAA has a form for almost everything. This wizard walks through the most common airport-engineering and aviation-ops scenarios and points to the right form, portal, or process.
Scenarios Covered
  • Navigable airspace construction / alteration (7460-1)
  • On-airport construction notice (7480-1)
  • ALP amendments & FAA coordination letters
  • Wildlife hazard reports (Part 139)
  • Aircraft registration (8050-1, 8050-2)
  • Airman change-of-address (8060-55)
  • Medical certificate renewal (MedXPress)
  • Pilot certificate application (8710-1 via IACRA)
  • Drone / sUAS registration (FAADroneZone)
  • Part 107 Remote Pilot (IACRA)
  • Accident / incident reporting (NTSB 6120.1)
  • Noise complaints (airport-specific)
  • Runway incursion & near-miss (NASA ASRS)
  • Part 135 / Part 121 operator certification
  • Part 139 annual update (5010-1)
  • Master plan / AIP grant applications
Filing Offices & Portals
  • ADO — Airports District Office (local)
  • FSDO — Flight Standards District Office
  • AJV-P51 — Obstruction Evaluation / Airport Airspace Analysis (OE/AAA)
  • CAMI — Civil Aerospace Medical Institute (OK City)
  • FAA Aircraft Registry — Oklahoma City
  • FAADroneZone — drone.faa.gov
  • IACRA — iacra.faa.gov
  • MedXPress — medxpress.faa.gov
  • NTSB — ntsb.gov (Form 6120.1)
  • NASA ASRS — asrs.arc.nasa.gov
Disclaimer
This is a planning aid from Korb Engineering, not legal or regulatory advice. Form numbers, office assignments, and filing procedures change. Always confirm with your FAA ADO, FSDO, or the current version of the form on faa.gov before submitting.
