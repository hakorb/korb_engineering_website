About This Tool

Converts between the most common geospatial coordinate systems used in civil and aviation engineering. All math is performed client-side using proj4.js 2.12.1.

  • WGS84 Lat/Long (EPSG:4326) — the datum used by GPS, Google Earth, and most GIS. Accepts decimal degrees or DMS.
  • UTM — Universal Transverse Mercator, 60 zones worldwide, meters. Specify zone 1-60 and N/S hemisphere.
  • US State Plane (NAD83) — 120+ zones, both US Survey Feet and meters where standard. Optimized for small-area projects with sub-meter accuracy.
  • Web Mercator (EPSG:3857) — the projection used by Google/Bing/OSM web maps. Meters, global extent.
