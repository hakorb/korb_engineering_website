Pile geometry

Shape factor is multiplied by base area × height.
Length × Width is the base; Height is the peak height above the ground.
Applied when computing volume from the GPS-walked outline.
Default 16 cy dump truck. Loose topsoil ≈ 1.35 ton/cy; dry sand ≈ 1.30; damp loam ≈ 1.45; gravel ≈ 1.50.
    How the math works
    Base area is computed from the entered dimensions (or the GPS polygon via the shoelace formula). Volume is area × height × shape factor. The result is converted to cubic feet, then divided by 27 to get cubic yards. Haul count is ceil(cubic yards ÷ truck capacity). Tonnage is cy × unit weight. GPS distances use the haversine formula on WGS-84 ellipsoid.
