    Nutrition Education

    Why 30/30/40 can work: roughly 30% protein helps muscle retention and satiety, 30% fat supports hormones and slower digestion, and 40% carbs can support training output and steadier daily energy.

    Label tips: check serving size first, compare sugar per serving to total servings, and scan ingredient order for hidden sugars (syrups, maltodextrin, juice concentrates).

    Myths: "fat makes you fat" is false in isolation, "all carbs are bad" is false, and "detox teas cleanse toxins" is mostly marketing.
