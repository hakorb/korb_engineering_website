    📚 Nutrition Education

    Why balanced macros work: Adequate protein supports muscle retention and satiety, healthy fats support hormones and slower digestion, and carbs fuel training output and steady energy.

    Label tips: Check serving size first, compare sugar per serving to total servings, and scan ingredient order for hidden sugars (syrups, maltodextrin, juice concentrates).

    Common myths: "Fat makes you fat" is false in isolation. "All carbs are bad" is false. "Detox teas cleanse toxins" is mostly marketing.
