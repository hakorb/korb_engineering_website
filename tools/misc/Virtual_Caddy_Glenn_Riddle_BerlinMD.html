How to Use on the Course

  1. Select your course, hole, and tee box.
  2. Tap Locate Me and allow GPS. If that fails, enter a manual distance.
  3. Tap Refresh Weather (or switch to manual weather).
  4. Check the recommended club and explanation, then use your judgment.
  5. Use Read My Distance & Club for an audio readout in windy or bright conditions.
Disclaimer: This caddy recommendation is a guide only. Your judgment, lie, hazards, and course conditions come first.
