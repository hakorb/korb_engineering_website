How to Play

Move camera: WASD / arrow keys / edge scroll / drag minimap

Zoom: mouse wheel or +/- keys

Select: left-click unit, or drag box. Shift-click adds.

Double-click a unit to select all of that type on screen.

Move / attack: right-click (desktop) or long-press (touch).

Build: select a villager, then click a building from bottom-right menu, then click the map.

Gather: right-click a forest, stone, gold, berry, deer, or water tile with villagers selected.

Train units: select a Keep or production building, click unit buttons.

Advance age: select your Keep, click "Advance Age" (requires 2 buildings of current age).

Victory: destroy all enemy Keeps, or build a Wonder and defend it 5 minutes.

Pause: ESC or the || button. Speed button cycles 0.5x / 1x / 2x / 4x.

Tip: keep villagers working! Idle villagers are wasted. Build houses before hitting pop cap.
