BOOK DIGITIZER

Scan physical books to PDF, auto-saved to the Korb Digital Lending Library.
QUICK START
1. Mount your phone securely above the book.
2. Tap START to begin a new scan session.
3. Pick a capture mode: Manual, Auto (hands-free page-flip detection), or Timer.
4. Flip pages; each capture becomes a PDF page.
5. Tap SAVE PDF to compile, name, and auto-upload to your library.
TIPS
• Use even, diffuse lighting — avoid hard shadows.
• Keep the book flat; press spine gently for flat pages.
• Auto-detect works best with consistent framing — don't move the camera.
• The final PDF is hard-capped at 30 MB; quality will auto-reduce if needed.
