Tips & Tricks

🎯 Accuracy First

Nail the handshape before speed. Keep movement clean and repeatable. Use a mirror or your phone's front camera for 5-minute form checks.

😊 Use Facial Grammar

Questions, emotions, and emphasis rely on your face in ASL. Practice WH questions with raised/furrowed eyebrows and natural mouth cues.

⏱️ Daily 10-Minute Routine

4 min new signs → 4 min review → 2 min sentence drill. Always end with your 3 weakest signs. Use spaced repetition: 1 day, 3 day, 7 day review.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Signing too low or outside signing space. Dropping non-manual signals. Rushing transitions between signs. Keep your signing space between waist and forehead.
