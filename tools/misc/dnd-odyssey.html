    ★ Quick Reference

    Conditions

    Blinded - Can't see. Auto-fail sight checks. Attacks have disadvantage, attacks against have advantage.
    Charmed - Can't attack charmer. Charmer has advantage on social checks.
    Deafened - Can't hear. Auto-fail hearing checks.
    Frightened - Disadvantage on ability checks/attacks while source is in sight. Can't willingly move closer.
    Grappled - Speed becomes 0. Ends if grappler is incapacitated or effect removes creature.
    Incapacitated - Can't take actions or reactions.
    Invisible - Impossible to see without magic/special sense. Advantage on attacks, attacks against have disadvantage.
    Paralyzed - Incapacitated, can't move or speak. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks have advantage; melee hits are crits.
    Petrified - Transformed to stone. Weight increases x10. Incapacitated, can't move/speak. Resistance to all damage.
    Poisoned - Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks.
    Prone - Disadvantage on attacks. Melee attacks against have advantage, ranged have disadvantage. Must use half movement to stand.
    Restrained - Speed 0. Attacks have disadvantage. DEX saves have disadvantage. Attacks against have advantage.
    Stunned - Incapacitated, can't move, can speak only falteringly. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks against have advantage.
    Unconscious - Incapacitated, can't move or speak. Drops held items, falls prone. Auto-fail STR/DEX saves. Attacks have advantage; melee crits within 5 ft.
    Exhaustion - 6 levels. 1: Disadvantage on checks. 2: Speed halved. 3: Disadvantage on attacks/saves. 4: HP max halved. 5: Speed 0. 6: Death.

    Actions in Combat

    Attack - Make one melee or ranged attack (more with Extra Attack feature).
    Cast a Spell - Cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action.
    Dash - Gain extra movement equal to your speed for the turn.
    Disengage - Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the turn.
    Dodge - Attacks against you have disadvantage. DEX saves have advantage. Lost if incapacitated or speed drops to 0.
    Help - Give an ally advantage on their next ability check or attack roll against a target within 5 ft.
    Hide - Make a DEX (Stealth) check to hide.
    Ready - Prepare an action to trigger on a specific condition. Uses your reaction.
    Search - Make a WIS (Perception) or INT (Investigation) check.
    Use an Object - Interact with a second object or use an object that requires an action.

    Resting

    Short Rest - At least 1 hour. Can spend Hit Dice to regain HP (roll HD + CON mod each).
    Long Rest - At least 8 hours (sleep 6, light activity 2). Regain all HP. Regain spent Hit Dice up to half total (minimum 1). Can't benefit from more than one long rest in 24 hours.

    Acid - Corrosive spray, black dragon breath
    Bludgeoning - Blunt force: hammers, falling, constriction
    Cold - Infernal chill, icy blast, white dragon breath
    Fire - Red dragon breath, fireball
    Force - Pure magical energy: magic missile, eldritch blast
    Lightning - Lightning bolt, blue dragon breath
    Necrotic - Withering, life-draining energy
    Piercing - Puncturing: arrows, fangs, spears
    Poison - Venomous stings, toxic gas
    Psychic - Mental assault
    Radiant - Holy fire, divine energy
    Slashing - Swords, axes, claws
    Thunder - Sonic boom, shatter
