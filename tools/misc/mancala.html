HOW TO PLAY
Pick up all the stones in a pit on your side. Sow them one-per-pit counter-clockwise, including your own store but skipping your opponent's store.

Free turn: Land your last stone in your own store.
Capture: Land your last stone in an empty pit on your side and capture both that stone and all stones in the opposite pit on the opponent's side.
Game ends when one player's side is empty. The other player sweeps their remaining stones into their store. Most stones wins.
