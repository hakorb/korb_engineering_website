How to Play

TUDOR-STYLE VIBRATING-FIELD ELECTRIC FOOTBALL

Choose a Play

Pick a play from the playbook panel. Each card shows the formation and routes. Run plays move the ball carrier forward; pass plays let you throw.

Position Your Players

Drag any player to reposition them on the field. Tap a player (without dragging) to rotate their base 45 degrees. The arrow shows which direction they will skitter when the field vibrates.

Flip the Switch

Press the big green FLIP SWITCH button (or Space on keyboard). The field starts buzzing and all players skitter along their base direction with random wobble — just like the real toy.

Watch the Action

On run plays, the ball carrier (marked with a yellow ring) tries to reach the end zone. On pass plays, tap anywhere on the field to throw the ball after the snap.

Scoring

Touchdown = 7 points. Field Goal = 3 points. Safety = 2 points for the defense. You get 4 downs to gain 10 yards for a first down.

Mobile Controls

Use the D-pad below the field to nudge the selected player. The center button rotates their base. Long-press and drag on the field for fine positioning.

What is this?

A simulation of the classic 1970s vibrating-field electric football game (Tudor / Coleco style). A green metal field buzzes, and plastic players on bristly bases skitter across it — whichever direction the base is pointed. The ball carrier has to reach the opposite end zone without being touched by a defender. Half the fun was the unpredictable chaos when you flipped the switch.

Setup phase

Drag any player to move them before the snap. Tap a player (no drag) to cycle their base orientation 45 degrees. The little arrow on each base shows the direction they will scoot when the field vibrates. Your goal is to point blockers downfield and stack them against the defenders path to your runner.

By default, HOME is offense (orange) and AWAY is defense (cyan). The yellow-ringed player is the ball carrier — tap CHOOSE RUNNER and then click any offensive player to hand him the ball.

Running a play

Press ⚡ FLIP SWITCH. The field starts buzzing, every player skitters along their base direction (with random jitter), and collisions push them around like real vibrating figures. The play ends when:

  • A defender touches the ball carrier — tackled where he stands
  • The ball carrier crosses the far goal line — touchdown (7 pts)
  • The runner goes out of bounds — play ends at that spot
  • 15 seconds of real-world buzzing — play dies (everyone runs out of steam)

On PASS plays, press FLIP SWITCH then tap any spot on the field — the runner throws and an orange ball streaks to that target. A defender in the flight path can intercept or bat it down.

Keyboard shortcuts

Space Flip Switch · K Kick/Punt · W Whistle · R Rotate · P Pass mode · U Run mode · S Stats · ? Tutorial

Season and Tournament

Use the tabs at the top to switch between Quick Play, Season Mode (8-game season with standings), and Tournament (single-elimination bracket). Your progress is saved locally.
