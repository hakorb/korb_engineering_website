How to play

Press SNAP ▶ to begin a play. You're on offense — the bright blinking dash in the left column is your ball carrier. The dimmer red dashes are defenders closing in.

Run mode (default): Use ▶ to sprint forward. Each yard advances the ball. Use ▲/▼ to dodge between rows. If a defender lands on your cell, you're tackled.

Pass mode: Toggle RUN / PASS before snap. After snap, press ▶ to throw — the ball streaks downfield. If it reaches the end zone line without being intercepted by a defender in its path, it's complete for a big gain.

Kick: Use KICK to attempt a field goal (from ~40 yards or closer of the end zone) or to punt. Strategy matters on 4th down.

Scoring: Advance 10 yards for a first down. Reach the opponent end zone for a 7-point touchdown. Four 2-minute quarters. Keyboard: arrow keys or WASD, Space to snap, K to kick, M to toggle run/pass.
