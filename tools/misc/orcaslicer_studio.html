💡 Quick Tips
• First layer too thick? Decrease Z-offset in your printer settings.
• Stringing? Increase retraction distance and check temp.
• Layer separation? Increase nozzle temp or slow outer wall speed.
• Elephant foot? Reduce initial layer flow or raise nozzle slightly.
• CF filament? You MUST use a hardened steel nozzle — brass will wear fast.
