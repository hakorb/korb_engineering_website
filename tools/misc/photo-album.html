[ NOTICE · HOW SHARING WORKS ]

This is a browser-local album on YOUR device. Because korb.engineering is a static site with no server, photos you upload here are not automatically shared with other visitors.

To share photos across devices or people, use the Export Album button to download a JSON file, then send that file to another person who can use Import Album to load it. For a single small photo you can also use Copy Share Link.

Photos auto-delete from your browser after 14 days. A cleanup pass runs every time you open this page and once per hour while it is open.
