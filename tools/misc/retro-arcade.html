HOW TO PLAY

Controls:

  • Arrow keys or on-screen D-pad to move
  • A button or SPACE = Fire / Action
  • B button or SHIFT = Jump / Secondary
  • P or PAUSE = Pause
  • ENTER or START = Start / Restart

Games:

  • Brick Smash: Bounce ball to break all bricks
  • Asteroid Field: Rotate ship, shoot rocks
  • Space Shooter: Vertical scroller, destroy waves
  • Ladder Climber: Climb to the top dodging barrels
  • Maze Muncher: Eat dots, avoid specters
  • Snake Pit: Eat food, don't crash
  • Block Drop: Stack falling blocks, clear lines
  • Pong Duel: First to 4 wins. Auto AI opponent
  • Galactic Invaders: Shoot descending aliens
  • Frog Hopper: Cross road and river safely
