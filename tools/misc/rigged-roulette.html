HOUSE RULES

European Roulette uses a wheel with 37 pockets numbered 0-36. The 0 is green; the remaining numbers alternate red and black.

Inside bets: Straight up (35:1), Split (17:1), Street (11:1), Corner (8:1), Line (5:1).

Outside bets: Red/Black, Odd/Even, Low/High pay 1:1. Dozens and Columns pay 2:1.

The single zero gives the house a theoretical edge of approximately 2.7%.
