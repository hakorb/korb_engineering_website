HOW TO PLAY

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.

GREEN - Letter is correct and in position.
GOLD - Letter is in word but wrong spot.
GRAY - Letter not in word.

DAILY mode gives everyone the same word each day.
HARD mode forces revealed hints in subsequent guesses.
