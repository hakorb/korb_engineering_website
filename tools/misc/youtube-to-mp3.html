How To Use
  1. Paste a YouTube URL in the input field above
  2. Click RECORD AUDIO
  3. In the browser dialog, select This Tab and click Share
  4. The video will begin playing and audio will be captured in real time
  5. Click STOP RECORDING or wait for the video to end
  6. Your audio file will download automatically as .mp3
